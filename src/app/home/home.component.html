Welcome

Welcome to FieldID!

This app was created to solve a real world problem by managing user tracking in a venue

Adding new users

You can add new users by going to 'Manage Users'. From there you can type in their details and upon registration a unique QR code will be created. The user's QR code gets scanned everytime they enter the venue

Scanner

You can use 'Scanner' to scan the user's QR code. After a QR code gets scanned the user's details get added in the 'Record'.

Record

Record keeps track of the users you have scanned sorting them by most to less recent
